# Zoom Out

This is a user-triggered, read-only map. Apply `../../references/skills-kernel.md` once, then inspect
only the area needed to explain:

- the relevant modules and their responsibilities;
- their callers and downstream consumers;
- state, data, and control flow across boundaries;
- where the area fits in the broader system;
- unresolved ownership or vocabulary gaps.

Use `CONTEXT.md` terminology when present. Return a compact map with source pointers and avoid
implementation, refactoring, or speculative redesign.
